FAQ

What roles do you specialize in?

Technology, product, operations, business, and leadership across multiple industries.

What is your typical time‑to‑shortlist?

Most roles receive a curated shortlist within 7–14 business days, depending on seniority and niche.

Do you offer a replacement guarantee?

Yes. Most engagements include a free replacement within a defined window. Terms vary by role and agreement.

Which engagement models do you support?

Contingency, retained/executive search, and RPO (embedded pods) depending on the complexity and volume.

What regions do you cover?

We hire across India and international markets for remote, hybrid, and on‑site roles.

How do you ensure candidate quality?

Structured screening, skills validation, and behavioral interviews mapped to the job's must‑haves.

What does pricing look like?

Transparent, role‑based pricing with competitive fees. We'll share a proposal after understanding your needs.
